OUR STORY

At the heart of hungrX is a story of real transformation. Our founder’s journey began at 96 kg, navigating diets, endless advice, and the frustration of mixed results. But one truth became clear: a calorie deficit is the path to lasting change.

HungrX was created to simplify this journey for you. With geo-targeted food suggestions tailored to your daily calorie needs, we make each meal an empowering step toward your goals.

This is your moment to reclaim the life you deserve. Join HungrX, and let’s walk this path together—one meal, one victory at a time.

HungrX uses your geolocation to find nearby restaurants, providing menus complete with calorie and nutrition info. See which meals fit your goals, so eating out becomes part of your progress, not a setback.

How can I keep up with my plan, even on a busy day?

HungrX tracks both home-cooked and restaurant meals, so you’re always aware of your intake without extra effort. Stay on course effortlessly, no matter where you eat.

How can I stay motivated when my weight goals feel distant?

HungrX’s Gamified Progress Journey turns your goal into an interactive experience. Track every milestone through visual progress, making weight management encouraging and rewarding.

HungrX provides real-time calorie guidance, showing how each meal option impacts your daily goals. No guessing—just clear insights that make reaching your ideal weight feel achievable.
